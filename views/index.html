Charge API

The Charge Service will be used by Lender/Conveyancers and Conveyancers to create Charges to be applied to the Land Register. There are two services that will be provided to Lender/Conveyancers and Conveyancers through which they can submit Charges.

  • An online transaction
  • This API

Expected process flow

1. Lender/Conveyancer creates a Charge POST /charge

The lender uses the Charge API to create a Charge. They provide information on the mortgage product to use, the borrowers to list on the Charge, and the property to apply the Charge against.

2. Borrowers digitally sign the Charge Sign your mortgage deed service

The Borrowers each use the new Sign your mortgage deed service to digitally sign the Charge. This digital signature will be attached to the charge and held securely on the Land Register once the Charge has been completed.

3. Lender/Conveyancer checks that all borrowers have signed GET /charge/:id

Lender/Conveyancers can use the /charge/:id endpoint to check the current status of a Charge. Once all borrowers listed have digitally signed the Charge then the Lender/Conveyancer can date the charge and submit it to the Land Registry for recording on the Land Register.

4. Lender/Conveyancer dates the charge POST /charge/:id/:date

Once all Borrowers have signed the Lender/Conveyancers can use the /charge/:id/:date endpoint to date a Charge. The Charge would then be submitted to the Land Registry for adding to the Land Register.
